AdoptLiving is a free platform for you to bring your choice dog and cat to your home. AdoptLiving is an NGO that helps domestic animals to be saved, fed and also taken care. AdoptLiving was initiated by Talwar and wants everyone to be part to save domestic animals. AdoptLiving also provides you free vaccine shots for your pet.



In India hundreds of animals wait in anticipation of loving homes. Many have been rescued from suffering on the streets from being abandoned, injured or just old. All are deserving of a forever home and the process is a lot easier than you think.



Adopting a animal, dog or cat, is not something to be undertaken lightly. Cats can live as long as 15 years and dogs upto 20 years.

We want to ensure that any animals it places for adoption go to people who will provide them with an appropriate, clean, safe, family environment, will feed them properly, take care of them when they are sick, and will – above all – give them love until the end of their days.



We want you to be part of AdoptLiving to save all domestic animals around you, as well across the nation.
